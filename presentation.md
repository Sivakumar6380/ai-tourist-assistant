## Slide 2: The Problem
**Why Do We Need SafeTravel AI?**

* **High Vulnerability:** Tourists often face safety threats, fake identity scams, and overpriced transport in unfamiliar areas.
* **Delayed Emergency Response:** Traditional SOS systems lack contextual data, leading to slow response times.
* **Lack of Proactive Safety:** Current systems only react *after* an incident happens, rather than preventing it.
* **Identity Fraud:** Verification of tourists and service providers (like taxis) is slow and easily forged.

---

## Slide 3: Our Solution
**Proactive. Intelligent. Decentralized.**

SafeTravel AI shifts the paradigm from **reactive** to **proactive** protection by combining 4 core pillars:
1. **Real-time Live GPS Tracking:** Continuous monitoring of tourist movement.
2. **AI-Based Risk Prediction:** Dynamic threat analysis based on location, time, and behavior.
3. **Blockchain Identity Verification:** Unforgeable digital passports for tourists.
4. **Intelligent Protection Hub:** A centralized command center for emergency SOS and automated check-ins.

---

## Slide 4: Real-Time AI Travel Assistant (Powered by Gemini 2.5 Flash)
**Your Personal 24/7 Safety Guardian**

* **Context-Aware:** Knows the user's live GPS coordinates, current hotel, active risk score, and nearby threat zones.
* **Instant Guidance:** Ask natural language questions like *"Am I safe here?"* or *"Where is the nearest police station?"*
* **Dynamic Threat Mapping:** AI cross-references user location with local emergency services and active weather alerts in real-time.
* **Fully Integrated:** Not a generic chatbot, but a highly personalized companion built on Google's Gemini 2.5 architecture.

---

## Slide 5: The Protection Hub (Core Features)
**A Suite of Smart Safety Tools**

* **Safe Check-in Scheduler:** A dead-man switch that automatically alerts emergency contacts if the user fails to check in before the timer expires.
* **Acoustic Voice Panic AI:** Constantly monitors for distress keywords (e.g., *"Help Me!"*) to trigger automatic SOS alerts.
* **Simulated Inertial Sensors:** Detects sudden shakes, falls, or abnormal inactivity (simulated smartwatch integration).
* **Fake Taxi Plate Scanner:** Instantly verifies vehicle registration against government databases to prevent transport scams.

---

## Slide 6: Blockchain Identity Verification
**Trustless & Immutable Security**

* **Secure Onboarding:** Tourist details (Passport, Visa) are hashed and stored on a decentralized ledger using SHA-256 encryption.
* **QR Code Passports:** Generates a unique, verifiable QR code for each tourist.
* **Instant Police Verification:** Authorities can scan the QR code to instantly pull immutable identity records, eliminating fake ID scams.
* **Tamper-Proof:** Any attempt to alter the identity data breaks the hash chain, immediately flagging the record as compromised.

## Slide 7: Admin Command Center
**For Authorities & Tourism Boards**

* **Live Tourist Heatmap:** Real-time visibility of all active tourists across the city.
* **Dynamic Geofencing:** Admins can draw high-risk crime zones (red) or safe zones (green) directly on the map.
* **Instant Incident Management:** Real-time dashboard showing active SOS alerts, overdue check-ins, and high-risk anomalies.
* **Data Analytics:** Visualizes risk distribution and alert frequencies over time to help authorities allocate police patrols efficiently.
